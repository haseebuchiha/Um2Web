UMTO

UMTO

Click on the buttons to open the collapsible content.

Metal, plastic, wires, Arduino, lots of resistors and diodes. Plus 2 batteries, recipe for a perfect Frankenstein. In this case, UMTO. It runs on tank style wheels, has a rod for a body, a cheap Android Tablet for a face, and pulley system for adjusting height of the face. (Wicked! Right?)

This is Model 1.0. But through constant upgrades it may be promoted to 2.0, maybe. . . .
